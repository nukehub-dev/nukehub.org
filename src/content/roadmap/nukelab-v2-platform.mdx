NukeLab v2.0 delivers a complete rewrite of the workspace platform on FastAPI, React 19, PostgreSQL 17, Redis, Celery, and Traefik. It supports role-based access control, per-user quotas, immutable audit logs, alert rules, maintenance windows, and 500+ passing tests.
