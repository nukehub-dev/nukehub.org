## Open Source

NukeHub itself operates under the [BSD-2-Clause license](https://opensource.org/licenses/BSD-2-Clause), and we stand on the shoulders of countless open-source projects. We thank the developers and maintainers of the libraries, frameworks, and tools that make our work possible.
